League Rules

📋 General
  • This is a 10-team, full-PPR (point per reception) league.
  • Regular season runs Weeks 1–13. Playoffs run Weeks 14–17.
  • The top 6 teams advance to the playoffs.
  • All roster moves must be made before the weekly NFL game lock.
🏈 Roster
  • Starting lineup: QB, RB, RB, WR, WR, TE, FLEX (RB/WR/TE), K, DST.
  • Bench: 6 spots.
  • IR spot: 1 spot (player must have official IR designation).
  • Maximum of 2 players from any single NFL team on your active roster.
🔁 Trades
  • Trade deadline is the end of Week 10.
  • All trades are reviewed by the commissioner for collusion. Obvious collusion results in a veto.
  • Trades process after a 24-hour review period unless vetoed.
  • You may not trade draft picks.
📥 Waivers
  • Waivers process every Wednesday at 4:00 AM ET.
  • Waiver priority is continuous rolling: successful claims move you to last priority.
  • Free agents (after waiver period) are first-come, first-served.
  • You may not drop a player during an active game.
🏆 Playoffs
  • Top 6 teams qualify for playoffs.
  • Seeds 1 and 2 receive a first-round bye.
  • Matchups: (3 vs 6) and (4 vs 5) in the first round.
  • Championship game is played in Week 17.
  • In the event of a playoff tie, the team with more total points for the season advances.

Voting Decisions

July 12, 2025 Approved (8–2) Switch to Full PPR Scoring

League voted to move from 0.5 PPR to full PPR starting in the 2025 season.

July 12, 2025 Approved (7–3) Add a 3rd Place Consolation Prize

A $75 prize will now be awarded to the third-place finisher to keep more teams engaged in the playoffs.

July 12, 2025 Approved (9–1) Increase Roster Size from 14 to 15

One bench spot was added to allow more roster flexibility and bye week management.

August 2, 2024 Approved (6–4) Move Trade Deadline from Week 9 to Week 10

Approved for the 2024 season. Owners felt Week 9 was too early given the number of bye weeks.

August 2, 2024 Approved (10–0) Allow IR Spot for Injured Players

Unanimous approval. One IR spot added for players with an official IR designation.
